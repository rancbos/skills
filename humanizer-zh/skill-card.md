## Description: <br>
Helps an agent edit or review Chinese text by identifying common AI-writing patterns and rewriting the text to sound more natural while preserving meaning and tone. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[liuxy951129-cpu](https://clawhub.ai/user/liuxy951129-cpu) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External users, editors, and writers use this skill to revise Chinese drafts that sound overly generated, promotional, formulaic, or generic. The skill is intended for quality editing and review, not for hiding AI assistance where disclosure is required. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Rewriting can accidentally change meaning, remove nuance, or introduce unsupported specifics. <br>
Mitigation: Review the rewritten text or diff before accepting changes, especially for professional, legal, academic, or publishing contexts. <br>
Risk: The skill could be misused to hide AI assistance where disclosure is required. <br>
Mitigation: Do not use it to bypass disclosure obligations in academic, professional, legal, publishing, or other governed settings. <br>
Risk: Sensitive documents may be exposed to the editing workflow if users provide confidential text. <br>
Mitigation: Use the skill only on documents the user is comfortable rewriting in the active agent environment. <br>
